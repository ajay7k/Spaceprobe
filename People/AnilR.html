Dr. Anil Narayan Raghav

About the Founder & Director

Prof. (Dr.) Anil Narayan Raghav is a distinguished space scientist, innovator, and mentor with over 16 years of research and teaching experience in space physics. He currently serves as an Associate Professor in the Department of Physics at the University of Mumbai, where he has guided several M.Sc. and Ph.D. scholars, many of whom are now pursuing successful careers in space science worldwide.

As the Founder and Director of SpaceProbe Pvt. Ltd., Dr. Raghav is spearheading initiatives that integrate cutting-edge space research, technology development, and public engagement. His vision is to make space weather forecasting, satellite data applications, and astronomy outreach more accessible to industries, academic institutions, and the public.

Vision through SpaceProbe

Under his leadership, SpaceProbe Pvt. Ltd. is building an ecosystem that connects advanced research with societal benefits through:

  • GIS and remote sensing applications.
  • Astronomy and science outreach programs.
